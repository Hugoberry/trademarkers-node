Trademark in Europe

EUIPO is the European Union Agency responsible for the registration of the European Union trade mark (EUTM) and the registered Community design (RCD), two unitary intellectual property rights valid across all EU Member States.

Many companies that want trademark protection in all member states of the European Union file their trademark applications at the EUIPO instead

Important Facts

After the formal examination at the European Union Intellectual Property Office the trademark will be published for opposition to allow third parties to oppose if they believe that the mark infringes on their brands.

The opposition period in European Union Intellectual Property Office is 3 months after publication.

If no oppositions arise and your trademark is formally approved, you can expect registration within 7 months.
