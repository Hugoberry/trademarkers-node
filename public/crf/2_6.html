§2.6    Trademark fees.

  1. Trademark process fees.
    1. Application filing fees.
      1. For filing an application on paper, per class—$600.00
      2. For filing an application through TEAS, per class—$400.00
      3. For filing a TEAS Reduced Fee (RF) application through TEAS under § 2.23, per class—$275.00
      4. For filing a TEAS Plus application through TEAS under § 2.22, per class—$225.00
      5. Additional processing fee under §§ 2.22(c) or 2.23(c), per class—$125.00
    2. Amendment to allege use.
      1. For filing an amendment to allege use under section 1(c) of the Act on paper, per class—$200.00
      2. For filing an amendment to allege use under section 1(c) of the Act through TEAS, per class—$100.00
    3. Statement of use.
      1. For filing a statement of use under section 1(d)(1) of the Act on paper, per class—$200.00
      2. For filing a statement of use under section 1(d)(1) of the Act through TEAS, per class—$100.00
    4. Extension of time for filing statement of use.
      1. For filing a request under section 1(d)(2) of the Act for a six-month extension of time for filing a statement of use under section 1(d)(1) of the Act on paper, per class—$225.00
      2. For filing a request under section 1(d)(2) of the Act for a six-month extension of time for filing a statement of use under section 1(d)(1) of the Act through TEAS, per class—$125.00
    5. Application for renewal of a registration fees.
      1. For filing an application for renewal of a registration on paper, per class—$500.00
      2. For filing an application for renewal of a registration through TEAS, per class—$300.00
    6. Renewal during grace period.
      1. Additional fee for filing a renewal application during the grace period on paper, per class—$200.00
      2. Additional fee for filing a renewal application during the grace period through TEAS, per class—$100.00
    7. Publishing mark under section 12(c).
      1. For filing to publish a mark under section 12(c) on paper, per class—$200.00
      2. For filing to publish a mark under section 12(c), per class through TEAS—$100.00
    8. New certificate of registration.
      1. For issuing a new certificate of registration upon request of registrant, request filed on paper—$200.00
      2. For issuing a new certificate of registration upon request of registrant, request filed through TEAS—$100.00
    9. Certificate of correction of registrant’s error.
      1. For a certificate of correction of registrant’s error, request filed on paper—$200.00
      2. For a certificate of correction of registrant’s error, request filed through TEAS—$100.00
    10. Disclaimer to a registration.
      1. For filing a disclaimer to a registration, on paper—$200.00
      2. For filing a disclaimer to a registration, through TEAS or ESTTA—$100.00
    11. Amendment of registration.
      1. For filing an amendment to a registration, on paper—$200.00
      2. For filing an amendment to a registration, through TEAS or ESTTA—$100.00
    12. Affidavit under section 8.
      1. For filing an affidavit under section 8 of the Act on paper, per class—$225.00
      2. For filing an affidavit under section 8 of the Act through TEAS, per class—$125.00
    13. Affidavit under section 15.
      1. For filing an affidavit under section 15 of the Act on paper, per class—$300.00
      2. For filing an affidavit under section 15 of the Act through TEAS, per class—$200.00
    14. Filing section 8 affidavit during grace period.
      1. Additional fee for filing a section 8 affidavit during the grace period on paper, per class—$200.00
      2. Additional fee for filing a section 8 affidavit during the grace period through TEAS, per class—$100.00
    15. Petitions to the Director.
      1. For petitions to the Director filed on paper—$200.00
      2. For petitions to the Director filed through TEAS—$100.00
    16. Petition to cancel.
      1. For filing a petition to cancel on paper, per class—$500.00
      2. For filing a petition to cancel through ESTTA, per class—$400.00
    17. Notice of opposition.
      1. For filing a notice of opposition on paper, per class—$500.00
      2. For filing a notice of opposition through ESTTA, per class—$400.00
    18. Ex parte appeal.
      1. For ex parte appeal to the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board filed on paper, per class—$300.00
      2. For ex parte appeal to the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board filed through ESTTA, per class—$200.00
    19. Dividing an application.
      1. Request to divide an application filed on paper, per new application created—$200.00
      2. Request to divide an application filed through TEAS, per new application created—$100.00
    20. Correcting deficiency in section 8 affidavit.
      1. For correcting a deficiency in a section 8 affidavit via paper filing—$200.00
      2. For correcting a deficiency in a section 8 affidavit via TEAS filing—$100.00
    21. Correcting deficiency in renewal application.
      1. For correcting a deficiency in a renewal application via paper filing—$200.00
      2. For correcting a deficiency in a renewal application via TEAS filing—$100.00
    22. Extension of time for filing notice of opposition under § 2.102(c)(1)(ii) or (c)(2).
      1. For filing a request for an extension of time to file a notice of opposition under § 2.102(c)(1)(ii) or (c)(2) on paper—$200.00
      2. For filing a request for an extension of time to file a notice of opposition under § 2.102(c)(1)(ii) or (c)(2) through ESTTA—$100.00
    23. Extension of time for filing notice of opposition under § 2.102(c)(3).
      1. For filing a request for an extension of time to file a notice of opposition under § 2.102(c)(3) on paper—$300.00
      2. For filing a request for an extension of time to file a notice of opposition under § 2.102(c)(3) through ESTTA—$200.00
  2. Trademark service fees.
    1. For printed copy of registered mark, copy only. Service includes preparation of copies by the Office within two to three business days and delivery by United States Postal Service; and preparation of copies by the Office within one business day of receipt and delivery to an Office Box or by electronic means (e.g., facsimile, electronic mail)—$3.00
    2. Certified or uncertified copy of trademark application as filed processed within seven calendar days—$15.00
    3. Certified or uncertified copy of a trademark-related official record—$50.00
    4. Certified copy of a registered mark, showing title and/or status:
      1. Regular service—$15.00
      2. Expedited local service—$30.00
    5. Certified or uncertified copy of trademark records, per document except as otherwise provided in this section—$25.00
    6. For recording each trademark assignment, agreement or other document relating to the property in a registration or application
      1. First property in a document—$40.00
      2. For each additional property in the same document—$25.00
    7. For assignment records, abstract of title and certification, per registration—$25.00
    8. Additional Fee for Overnight Delivery—$40.00
    9. Additional Fee for Expedited Service—$160.00
    10. For processing each payment refused (including a check returned "unpaid") or charged back by a financial institution—$50.00
    11. Deposit account service charge for each month when the balance at the end of the month is below $1,000—$25.00
