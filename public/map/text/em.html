Trademark in Europe

EUIPO is the European Union Agency responsible for the registration of the European Union trade mark (EUTM) and the registered Community design (RCD), two unitary intellectual property rights valid across all EU Member States.

Many companies that want trademark protection in all member states of the European Union file their trademark applications at the EUIPO
