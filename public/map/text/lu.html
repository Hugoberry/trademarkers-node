Trademark in Benelux

Benelux, is a politico-economic union and formal international intergovernmental cooperation of three neighbouring states: Belgium, the Netherlands, and Luxembourg.

Many companies that want trademark protection in all member states of the European Union file their trademark applications at the EUIPO

Trademark in Benelux

Filing a trademark application in Luxembourg via the Benelux Office for Intellectual Property (BOIP) grants protection in Belgium, Netherlands and Luxembourg.
